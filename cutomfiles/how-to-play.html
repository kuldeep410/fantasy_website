T11 Regular

Create a team of handpicked & top-performing players and get set go for the match

  • Select the match you want to play.
  • Create your team of 11 players on both sides using 100 credits.
  • Choose a captain and a vice-captain.
  • Captain gets twice the points scored.
  • The Vice-captain gets one and half times the points scored.
  • Choose the contest from an available extensive range.
  • Click 'Preview' for a final check and join the cash contest.
  • Click on 'My Contest' to view the ranking and players' performance.

T11 Reverse

Identify the least-scoring players, create the team and aim for the minimum possible points to win the game

  • Tap on the match you want to play.
  • Select T11Reverse.
  • Choose 11 players from both teams with 100 credits.
  • Choose a captain and a vice-captain.
  • Choose the contest from a wide range.
  • Click on 'preview' for a final check and join the cash contest.
  • Click on 'My Contest' to view the ranking and players' performance.
